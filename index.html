How It Works

Step 1: Cleanse

Start with a clean face. Use a gentle cleanser to remove makeup and dirt. Pat dry with a towel. Suitable for day and night use.

Step 2: Apply Serum

Take a small amount of the serum and apply it evenly on your face and neck. Use circular motions to massage it in. Use in the morning for hydration and at night for repair.

Step 3: Moisturize

Massage a small amount of the moisturizer and let the product absorb fully into your skin before applying other skincare or makeup. Ideal for both day and night use.
